FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before you sign up.

When does the app launch?

We're launching in Boston, MA soon — starting with Boston College, Harvard, BU, Northeastern, Tufts, and Wellesley. Join the waitlist and we'll email you the moment it's live.

What schools are supported?

At launch we're starting with Boston College, Harvard, Boston University, Northeastern, Tufts, and Wellesley. More Boston-area schools will follow shortly after.

How do students get paid?

Students connect a bank account through the app (powered by Stripe) and receive direct deposits after each completed job. The minimum rate is $18/hr — always visible before you apply.

Is it safe? How are students verified?

Every student account requires a valid .edu email address, tying each profile to a real enrolled student. Both students and residents rate each other after every job, and the app has built-in reporting and blocking tools.

How much does it cost residents to post a job?

Your first job is always free to post. After that, you can pay small per-job fees or subscribe for unlimited postings at $20/month or $200/year. Fees are never charged upfront — only after a job is completed.

What kinds of jobs can I post or find?

Babysitting, tutoring, yard work, moving help, pet care, cleaning, errands, tech help, and more. If it's a task a college student can help with, it belongs on JobsU.
