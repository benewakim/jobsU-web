Why JobsU

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Built specifically for the college community — simple, safe, and designed for real people.

📍

Hyper-local

Jobs are matched to students at nearby colleges. Residents set a radius so only local students can apply.

⭐

Ratings & reviews

Both students and residents rate each other after every job. Trust is built into every interaction.

💬

Built-in messaging

Coordinate details directly in the app. No need to share phone numbers until you're ready.

🔔

Instant alerts

Students get notified the moment a matching job is posted. Residents get notified when accepted.

🛡️

Safe & verified

Every account is tied to a real email. Report and block tools keep the community safe.

💰

Transparent pay

Minimum $18/hr guaranteed. Students see exactly what they'll earn before they apply.
