Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

JobsU ("we," "us," or "our") operates the JobsU mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you create an account or use the app:

  • Name, email address, and password
  • Profile information (school, bio, photo, GPA, job preferences)
  • Address and location data (for residents setting a job radius)
  • Payment information (processed securely via Stripe)
  • Messages sent within the app
  • Ratings and reviews you submit

We also collect certain information automatically:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Push notification tokens (for job alerts)
  • App usage and interaction data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To match students with relevant local job postings
  • To facilitate communication between residents and students
  • To process payments through our payment provider (Stripe)
  • To send push notifications about job matches and application updates
  • To display ratings and reviews on profiles
  • To investigate reports of policy violations
  • To improve the app and fix bugs

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in these circumstances:

  • With other users: Your profile information (name, school, rating, bio, photo) is visible to other app users when you apply for jobs or post jobs.
  • With Stripe: Payment information is handled by Stripe, Inc. We do not store full payment card details. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • With service providers: We use Supabase for database and authentication services.
  • For legal reasons: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure with row-level security policies. Authentication tokens are stored in your device's encrypted keychain. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We strive to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications through your device settings

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@jobsu.app.

6. Children's Privacy

JobsU is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date above.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
support@jobsu.app
